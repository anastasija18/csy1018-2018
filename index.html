Introduction

You’d think it’s simple enough to write an introduction stating the key elements of it. Proven difficult even though I produced the entire thing! The purpose of this website is to educate readers and express our, students, knowledge is an accessible manner. Anyway, this newspaper is all about web development and web developers, the jobs they do, comparing a few of them to give an insight of the differences there are. Within a page, there is reviews based on current web related resources. An attempt was made to create a useful glossary containing any terminology in relation to web development. Outsiders may refer to those terms as ‘gibberish’ but I strived to make it as English as possible. It’d be pointless to have just a few terms in the glossary and no demonstrations of what they are- so, on one of the webpages there is a tutorial presenting how to apply the action to your own work.
